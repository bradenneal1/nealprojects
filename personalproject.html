Personal Project

A ski/tramping rental house in National Park Village designed for short stay small groups of people.

The site has been chosen, and designs drawn.

Updates

The site being a layer of peat, 34 large poles were rammed into the ground. A solid base was found 4 - 4.5m down.

Under floor heating tubes tied to the steel mesh. All ready for the concrete pour.

A steel framework anchors the building together.

 

Exterior cladding is finished using wood and steel materials.
